About

Welcome to Jina's digital storage box for her birthday card collection!
Here are some things to keep in mind when peeking through her collection:

-Click open windows to take a closer look at the birthday cards they hold

-Click the exit buttons on the windows as you would on a normal computer to view all the cards at once.
Note: changes to exit buttons occur on a mobile device

Have fun peeking!
